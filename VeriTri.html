How VeriTri Works

1. Report

Students can report a lost or found item by providing the necessary information and uploading a photo.

2. Verification

The administrator reviews every report to ensure that all information is accurate before it becomes visible.

3. Claim

The rightful owner submits a claim. After verification, the administrator schedules the return of the item.
